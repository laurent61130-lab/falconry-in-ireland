How to Find Us

Situated on the Clonmacnoise Road near the picturesque village of Shannonbridge, we are easily accessible from the major towns of the Irish Midlands.

From Dublin

Take the M6 west towards Athlone, then head south on the N62 through Ballinasloe. Follow signs for Shannonbridge. Approx. 1 hr 45 min from Dublin.

From Galway

Head east on the M6, exit at Ballinasloe and take the N62 south. Continue through Ferbane to reach Shannonbridge. Approx. 1 hr 10 min from Galway.

From Limerick

Take the N7 north, then the N62 through Roscrea and Birr, following signs for Shannonbridge along the Shannon. Approx. 1 hr 20 min from Limerick.
