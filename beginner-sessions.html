What to Expect

A Hands-On Introduction to Falconry

Our beginner sessions are crafted for those who've never held a raptor before. In just a few hours, you'll go from curious visitor to active falconer — glove on, bird in flight, heart racing.

Led by our experienced, UNESCO-aware falconers, every session takes place in the stunning natural landscapes of Co. Offaly, combining heritage, wildlife, and pure Irish countryside magic.

Whether you're a solo traveller, a couple on a weekend escape, or a family searching for something truly memorable — this is the experience that stays with you.

Session Highlights

What's Included in Your Experience

  • Glove & Equipment Fitting

    Your session begins with a proper fitting of the traditional falconer's gauntlet and a safety briefing tailored to absolute beginners.

  • Heritage & History Introduction

    Learn about Ireland's falconry heritage, its UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage status, and the ancient bond between humans and raptors.

  • Free-Flight Demonstration

    Watch our expert falconers demonstrate free-flight techniques before you take part. Witness the raw power and grace of a bird in full flight.

  • Fly a Bird of Prey Yourself

    The highlight of every session — call a trained raptor to your glove. Feel the wind from its wings and forge an unforgettable connection with nature.

  • Photography Opportunities

    Capture the moment. Our falconers are happy to pause for photographs, ensuring you leave with stunning memories of your time with the birds.

  • Meet Multiple Species

    Get up close with Harris Hawks, Barn Owls, and other resident raptors. Learn about each species, their behaviours, and their role in Irish falconry tradition.

Practical Information

Everything You Need to Know

  • Duration

    Sessions run approximately 1 to 2 hours, depending on group size and weather conditions.

  • Location

    Clonmacnoise Road, Shannonbridge, Co. Offaly — set in the beautiful Shannon Callows, a rich habitat for birds of prey.

  • Age & Fitness

    Suitable for ages 8 and above. No special fitness required. Sessions take place on flat, accessible ground.

  • Weather Policy

    Sessions run in all but the most severe weather. We recommend layers and waterproof footwear — this is Ireland, after all!

  • Advance Booking Required

    Sessions must be booked in advance to guarantee your place. Get in touch via phone or email to check availability.

  • Group Sizes

    Standard sessions accommodate up to 8 participants. For larger groups or private bookings, see our Private & Group Bookings page.
