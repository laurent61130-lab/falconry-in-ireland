Why Falconry in Ireland?

More Than an Activity —
A Living Tradition

Falconry was granted UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage status in 2016, recognising its unique bond between humans and birds of prey. In Ireland, this ancient art has been practised for over a thousand years — shaped by the landscapes, monasteries, and noble houses of this island.

Our experiences are designed not merely as activities, but as genuine encounters with this living heritage. Our licensed falconers share their knowledge, their birds, and their deep respect for the natural world with every guest.
