A Living Tradition

Over 4,000 Years of Bond Between Raptor & Human

Falconry is one of humanity's oldest partnerships — a dialogue of trust between falconer and bird, refined over millennia. In Ireland, this art has flourished amid rolling green hills, ancient bogs, and mist-laden mornings, weaving itself into the cultural fabric of the land.

Recognised by UNESCO as Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity, falconry is not merely a sport — it is a philosophy, a way of reading nature, and a bridge between past and present.
